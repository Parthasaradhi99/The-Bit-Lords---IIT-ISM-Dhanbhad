Get Started

Traditional attendance systems can be tedious and error-prone.

Our website provides a modern and efficient approach to attendance tracking.

Professors only need to provide an Excel sheet of student names, pictures, and camera IP address.

Facial recognition technology automatically detects and marks attendance.

Manual tracking is eliminated, minimizing the risk of errors.

Professors can streamline their attendance process and focus on teaching.

Are you a teacher or a student
